About Me

I'm Mateus, 17 years old, currently in my final year of secondary school at Escola Avelar Brotero. I discovered a deep passion for art at the age of 12 and have been pursuing the dream of becoming an artist ever since. Art has always been my natural way of expressing myself, and over time, it became not just a hobby, but a life path.

My greatest inspirations come from online artists, especially Brazilians like Saiko, Drawn Mask, and Nerdzito, who showed me how it's possible to transform creative ideas into powerful visual narratives. Internationally, artists like Hayao Miyazaki, master of colorful and sensitive animation, and Yusuke Murata, known for his detailed and monochromatic scenes, shaped two extremes that fascinate me: the vibrant and the restrained, chaos and calm — and I try to explore this duality in my works.

My artistic signature — Serumano or Serumas — holds personal meaning. I used this nickname in my early teens with friends, and although I no longer use it daily, I decided to keep it alive in my works as a tribute to that important phase of my life.
